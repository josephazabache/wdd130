Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to share our Peruvian culture with the world. Share recipes, our customs and the beauty of our country.

Audience

We want to give the best information about Peruvian culture. Show the world the geographical and culinary wonders that exist in this Latin American country. Over time we want to cover most of the customs that exist in this beautiful polycultural country. We believe that Peru has become a very recognized and famous country based on its culture, and we know that many people want to know about it, and know what it is like. Being a tourist destination increasingly visited by people from all over the world, we want to provide help, to be able to answer as many questions about culture or food. Peruvian culture is very varied, from the north to the south it is all very different, having a coast, mountains and jungle, the communities, the cities, the people are very different, each one with customs that they have acquired for many years. before. The country has great wonders like Macchu Pichhu, the Nazca lines, the oldest civilization in America like Caral, and many more. Whether they have visited the country or not, we know that many want to know not only about the culture but also how to prepare the delicious typical dishes of the country. It is new to no one that Peru is one of the most important culinary destinations in the world, due to all its diversity, There are dishes of all kinds of flavor, color, etc. We can provide you with the recipes and the necessary information so that you can do it, so that you can prepare a typical meal as similar to traditional food. Being a page created and managed by Peruvians, we can give the most accurate information, the best advice for any questions you may have, our goal is to help and make Peruvian culture better known and shared than it is.
